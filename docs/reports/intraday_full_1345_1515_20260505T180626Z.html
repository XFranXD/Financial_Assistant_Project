METRIC GUIDE

Confidence Score (System 1) Market signal strength 0-100. Measures momentum, volume, sector alignment, and risk. Strong ≥70 · Moderate 50-69 · Weak <50.
EQ Score (System 2) Earnings quality 0-100. Measures how well reported profits are backed by real cash flows. PASS ≥75 · WATCH 55-74 · FAIL <55.
EQ Verdict SUPPORTIVE — earnings back the signal. NEUTRAL — acceptable, monitor. WEAK — minor concerns. RISKY — earnings do not support signal. UNAVAILABLE — no SEC filing data.
Rotation Signal (System 3) Sector timing signal. SUPPORT — sector flow favors acting now. WAIT — neutral timing. WEAKEN — sector flow deteriorating. UNKNOWN — insufficient data.
Entry Quality (System 4) Price structure entry timing. GOOD — near support or confirmed base, valid entry window. EXTENDED — too far above support, pullback risk. EARLY — pattern forming, not confirmed. WEAK — no valid setup. Required GOOD for BUY NOW.
Move Extension % above the 126-day low. Higher values mean price has already moved significantly from its base. Used to detect extended entries where pullback risk is elevated.
Structure State TRENDING — price in directional move. CONSOLIDATING — price compressing in a range (base-building). VOLATILE — erratic, no clear structure.
Dist. to Support / Resistance % distance from current price to nearest support and resistance levels. Tells you where price sits in its current range.
Combined Reading Deterministic four-layer synthesis of all systems. ALIGNED ≥ 2 systems agree. PARTIAL — mixed. CONFLICT — systems disagree. BUY NOW requires entry_quality = GOOD with all other gates met.
Trend (1M/3M/6M) Price return over 1, 3, and 6 months. Positive across all three = confirmed momentum. One timeframe only = early stage.
Risk Score Composite risk measure 0-100. Higher = more risk. Factors: debt, volatility, liquidity, earnings stability, drawdown.
Event Risk (Phase 1A) NORMAL — no earnings or macro event imminent. HIGH RISK — earnings within 1–5 days or Fed/CPI release within 1 day. HIGH RISK blocks BUY NOW regardless of entry quality.
Insider Activity (Phase 1B) Directional signal from SEC Form 4 filings (90-day window). ACCUMULATING — net insider buying. DISTRIBUTING — net insider selling. NEUTRAL — no clear direction. UNAVAILABLE — no filing data. Confirmation layer only — does not change alignment score.
R/R Ratio (Phase 1C) Risk/Reward = (Target − Entry) ÷ (Entry − Stop). Minimum threshold is 2.0x. Below 2.0x overrides entry_quality to WEAK automatically. Stop is placed 0.5% below nearest support level.

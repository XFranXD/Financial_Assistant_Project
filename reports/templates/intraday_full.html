METRIC GUIDE

Confidence Score (System 1) Market signal strength 0-100. Measures momentum, volume, sector alignment, and risk. Strong ≥70 · Moderate 50-69 · Weak <50. Does NOT measure earnings quality.
EQ Score (System 2) Earnings quality 0-100. Measures how well reported profits are backed by real cash flows. Independent of market signal. PASS ≥75 · WATCH 55-74 · FAIL <55.
EQ Verdict SUPPORTIVE — earnings back the signal. WEAK — minor concerns, monitor closely. RISKY — earnings do not support the signal. UNAVAILABLE — insufficient SEC filing data.
Combined Reading Deterministic synthesis of System 1 market signal and System 2 earnings quality. Use this as your starting point for further research — it does not replace independent analysis.
Trend (1M/3M/6M) Price return over 1, 3, and 6 months. Positive across all three = confirmed momentum. One timeframe only = early stage.
Risk Score Composite risk measure 0-100. Higher = more risk. Factors: debt, volatility, liquidity, earnings stability, drawdown.
ROIC Return on Invested Capital — profit generated per dollar invested in the business. Above 15% = excellent. Below 8% = weak.
Drawdown How far the stock has fallen from its 90-day highest price. Above 20% = meaningful risk even if fundamentals look clean.
